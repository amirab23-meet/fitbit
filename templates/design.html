What's a FitBit?:

Fitbit is a consumer electronics and fitness company. It produces wireless-enabled wearable technology,physical fitness monitors and activity trackers such as smartwatches, pedometers and monitors for heart rate, quality of sleep and stairs climbed as well as related software.



Is it worth getting a Fitbit?:

Most of the devices can tell you exactly how far you ran, how high your heart rate goes during a workout, how active you've been during a day and helps improve your sleep. so If you match our persona and care about your health , physique and your mental health , a Fitbit is the perfect thing for you.
