What features does a FitBit offer?


1.tracking your sleeping patterns to give you advice on how to enhance it.

2.monitoring and tracking your heart rate.


3.keeping track of the level of oxygen in your blood.


4.GPS tracking during exercise.


5.storing and listening to music on the watch.


6.receiving call and text notifications also on the watch.


7.paying for items when out and about just by linking your card.

For a Bicep Workout Click Here!
